Japan - a tribute page

Overview

Japan is an island country in East Asia, located in the northwest Pacific Ocean.

The country comprises an archipelago of 6,852 islands; the five main islands are Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu, Okinawa.

Facts:

  • Capital city: Tokyo
  • National language: Japanese
  • Population: 125.9 million
  • Area: 377.9km2
  • GDP: $5.41 trillion
  • Currency: Japanese yen (¥)

My experience

I visited Japan in May/June 2017 with my girlfriend and we immediately fell in love with the country.

We started our journey in Tokyo and stayed there for several nights, with a day trip to Kamakura fit in. We then moved on to historic Kyoto, taking in the Japan of old. From Kyoto we moved down to Hiroshima briefly, and then on to the island of Miyajima. From there we went to the mountainous region of Koya San, staying in a monastery for the night. We then left for Osaka for the last part of our journey, even manaing to fit a day trip to Nara to see the deer and old temples. Finally we flew out of Osaka, exhauseted but happy due to the incredible experience we had over a two week period.

As money was tight (Japan can be a very expensive place!) we stayed in small Airbnb apartments for the most part of the trip - even though it is technically banned...

The weather was also incredible when we visited. We arrived right after the cherry blossom season, so it was coming into summer. We were blessed with 25 degrees C for most days and it rarely rained.
